# Diving deeper into the party life of New York in 2016 through noise complaints.

In the year of 2026, it's the year of nostalgia for the aesthetic of the 2016 era. The party scene of New York created some defining moments of 2016's pop culture. 

[Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/somesnm/partynyc)

Today, we look at this dataset from Kaggle. The dataset here gives us a look at how late into the night parties were called for noise complaints and which areas of New York were most frequently called on. 
While Kaggle is the hosting platform, the data originally comes from the New York City Campaign Finance Board, an independent, nonpartisan government agency. They collect campaign finance disclosures from candidates running for NYC offices. The dataset is _fairly trustworthy_
